## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 About This Policy

USMSB SDK ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our software development kit (SDK), application programming interfaces (APIs), and related services (collectively, the "Services").

### 1.2 Who We Are

USMSB SDK is a software development toolkit provided by [Company Name], located at [Address]. We act as the data controller for the personal information collected through our Services.

### 1.3 Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:
- Developers and organizations using USMSB SDK
- End users of applications built with USMSB SDK (as applicable)
- Visitors to our website and developer portal
- Participants in our community forums and events

### 1.4 Compliance

We are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws, including:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - United States
- Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL) - China
- Other applicable regional and national privacy laws

|-----------|------------------|--------|

- Through your account settings

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

#### Technical Measures
- **Encryption**: TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest

- **Network Security**: Firewalls, intrusion detection, VPN
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring, SIEM
- **Vulnerability Management**: Regular scanning, penetration testing

#### Organizational Measures
- **Security Policies**: Comprehensive security framework
- **Training**: Regular security awareness training
- **Incident Response**: Documented procedures, rapid response
- **Vendor Management**: Security assessments, contracts

### 9.2 Certifications

We maintain:
- SOC 2 Type II certification
- ISO 27001 certification (in progress)
- GDPR compliance documentation

### 9.3 Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a data breach:
1. We assess and contain the breach
